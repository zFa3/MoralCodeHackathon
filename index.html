Welcome to EcoEats!

Tired of seeing perfectly good food being thrown out? Wanting to manage your budget and
have your own personal AI constantly creating analytics and aiding you? You've came to the right place!

EcoEats is an all in one application used for tracking, reducing, analyzing, and preventing food waste
in your daily life. With the app, you can easily log in your foods, track consumption, and discover creative
ways of reducing food waste!



Why use EcoEats?

  • Reduce Waste: Say goodbye to wasting large amounts of food. EcoEats will make the most of every single ingredient in your home!
  • Save Money By optimizing and analyzing food consumption and shopping habits, you'll be saving money consistently and minimizing unnecessary purchases!
  • Save the Planet Every meal is important. When you reduce your food waste, you are contributing to ensuring the health of your planet.



How does it work?

  1. Log Your Food: Easily input current foods and ingredients in your home and we'll do the rest!
  2. Track Consumption: Monitor food usage and waste with simple diagrams and graphs.
  3. Get Personalized Reccomendations: recieve suggestions from a specialized AI that uses machine learning and is specified to your preferences to get meal ideas, recipes, and more based off your ingredients.
  4. Give Us Feedback: Send us feedback on our page and we'll use it to improve the effectiveness of the application
  5. Recieve Rewards: Recieve special coupons for popular food industries as rewards for reducing your food waste!
